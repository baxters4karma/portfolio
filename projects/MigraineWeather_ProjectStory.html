Correlation Analysis Between Migraine Incidences and Weather Patterns

Leveraging Big Data to Unveil Environmental Triggers for Migraines

Project Summary

This capstone project delves into the complex interplay between atmospheric conditions and migraine occurrences, focusing specifically on the correlation with sea-level pressure changes. The goal is to provide actionable insights for healthcare professionals and policymakers through comprehensive data analysis and visualization techniques.

Project Story

My journey in this project began with an exploration of extensive weather and health data. By employing Python and its powerful libraries, along with Jupyter Notebooks for thorough documentation, I navigated through large datasets to discern patterns and correlations. The project not only involved sophisticated data manipulation and visualization but also rigorous statistical testing to substantiate the findings.

Problem/Question to be Solved

My primary objective was to unravel whether fluctuations in sea-level pressure could be linked to the prevalence of migraines. This exploration aimed at enhancing our understanding of environmental factors influencing health, particularly in migraine management.

Process of Getting to the Resolution

  1. Data Acquisition:
    Sourcing data from meteorological and healthcare databases, including over 27 million data points on weather patterns and 1.4 million data points on migraine occurrences.
  2. Data Preparation and Cleaning:
    Rigorous data cleaning and preprocessing using Python to ensure data quality and relevance for analysis.
  3. Exploratory Data Analysis:
    Employing descriptive and inferential statistical techniques to unearth potential correlations between weather patterns and migraine frequencies.
  4. Statistical Testing and Validation:
    Using advanced statistical methods to validate the significance of the observed correlations.
  5. Insights and Implications:
    Translating complex data insights into practical recommendations for mitigating the impact of migraines.

Technologies Used

  • Python and its libraries for data processing and visualization.
  • Jupyter Notebooks for detailed documentation of the data analysis journey.
  • Advanced statistical tools for hypothesis testing and data exploration.
